# Low-Barrier Channels

These channels are easier to start with than Reddit-style communities. The safest framing is tool sharing, tutorial writing, or asking for feedback. Avoid repeated copy-paste comments.

## Avoid or Use Carefully

- Reddit: many subreddits dislike self-promotion and new accounts get filtered easily.
- Blind spam comments: high ban risk and bad brand signal.
- Telegram/Discord communities: only mention the tool when the discussion is directly about diagrams, docs, Mermaid, PlantUML, or AI-generated technical docs.

## First Posting Order

1. V2EX
2. 掘金 article
3. 博客园 or CSDN SEO post
4. Dev.to or Hashnode tutorial
5. Indie Hackers build-in-public note
6. Hacker News Show HN after the homepage, AI tools, and export flow feel polished
